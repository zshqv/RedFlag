Severity Logic

  • HIGH: Sentiment < -0.25 or keyword in always-high list (e.g., going concern, material weakness).
  • MEDIUM: Sentiment in [-0.25, -0.10) or mitigating language present.
  • LOW: Sentiment ≥ -0.10 with no amplifying language.

Severity Score: Base 50 + 20 (HIGH) − 15 (mitigating) + 15 (amplifying) + 10 (co-occurrence). Clamped 0–100.
